XYZ evint

XYZ Events plans to develop an innovative Event Management System (EMS) to streamline event organization, enhance resource allocation, improve team communication, and boost attendee engagement. The EMS will feature a user-friendly interface with a navigation bar and footer for easy navigation. Organizers will create accounts with personal information and manage events with detailed specifications such as name, date, location, capacity, and description. The system will display a list of all events to help organizers avoid scheduling conflicts. Attendees can register by providing personal details, selecting events, and choosing ticket types with associated prices, ensuring accurate management of attendee information and seamless linkage to respective events.
